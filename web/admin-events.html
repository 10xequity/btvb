Events & Programs

Drag a template chip onto a day to create an event there, or drag an existing event to a different day to reschedule it (its time of day is kept).

A view controls what the public schedule shows. Public hides names and counts; Internal shows both. Custom views get an unguessable link — settings are enforced by the server, not just hidden in the page.
