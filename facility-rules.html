Safety policies

Please read before you arrive.

Before you enter

  • Boomtown FieldhouseUSA is a private facility; we may refuse entry or ask anyone to leave for any reason.
  • By entering you accept these rules and the waiver terms — this includes players and spectators. You enter at your own risk.
  • Photos and video taken in the facility belong to the facility and may be used in marketing. Security cameras and staff monitor the building.
  • Boomtown provides the venue only and is not responsible for the organization of external tournaments/events or for injuries, damages, or losses during them — contact your tournament organizer with event questions.

Bags & weapons

  • No weapons or firearms of any kind.
  • Only player backpacks are allowed inside. Purses and camera bags are permitted but must be open for a quick check.

Food & drink

  • No outside commercial food or drink — including Starbucks/coffee. No coolers of food.
  • No gum. No sports drinks, coffee, or tea in playing areas (slip and delay hazards).
  • Only sealed, clear water is allowed on the courts. No Stanley/uncapped cups.
  • No eating or snacks on the courts. A bar & café is on site; all alcohol stays in the bar seating area.
  • If you bring outside food, eat it inside your vehicle.

Courts & equipment

  • Don’t climb nets, fences, or sprinklers, stand on tables/bar tops, or enter non-play areas — offenders pay for damages.
  • No personal chairs, camping chairs, coolers, or self-carried team tables on the courts. Don’t move court chairs.
  • Floor mats and seats are fine if they don’t scratch the floor. Movable spectator chairs are first-come, first-served — please return them.
  • No ball handling or play (including dribbling/throwing) outside authorized event areas.
  • Clean spills immediately — the responsible team stops play to clean up. Each team must clean its area after playing or be fined.

Kids & restricted areas

  • Children must be supervised at all times or will be asked to leave.
  • Stay out of other athletic or under-construction areas — soccer field, lounge, med-spa, gymnastics, and dance studios.

Pets

  • No animals except service animals as defined by law. Please give at least 12 hours’ written notice.
  • Service animals are kept off all playing surfaces and activity areas.

First aid & power

  • There is no athletic trainer or ice on site — bring your own. First-aid kits/bandages can be purchased for a fee.
  • No appliances may be plugged in. Phone charging is allowed around the bar areas only.

Parking & out front

  • Parking is free and plentiful, but we are not responsible for your vehicle — lock it and keep valuables out of sight.
  • No tables, camping, or tailgating in front of the building (Aurora camping ban; other businesses operate here). Unattended items are removed.

Lost & found

  • There is no lost & found — unclaimed items are disposed of nightly for sanitation. Lost electronics/valuables are not our responsibility. Please don’t call or email about lost items.

USAV RMR events

Food policy & logistics for sanctioned RMR play.

  • RMR players only may bring a brown-bag lunch carried in a player backpack; if it doesn’t fit in the backpack, it isn’t allowed. Spectators may not bring bags (except purses/camera bags) or food.
  • Doors open at 7:00 AM; team areas are first-come, first-served. Coaches meeting is at 7:30 AM at the admin desk.
  • Warm-up balls/carts are not provided (RMR provides one game ball per court). Guest Wi-Fi is available.
  • A limited number of court-approved folding chairs are provided — return them to the rack at the end.
  • Coaches/directors must check out with the site director before leaving or final scores won’t be entered (automatic forfeit).

Spectators & fines

Repeat violations are monitored on camera.

  • Spectators who refuse to comply will be asked to leave or escorted out by APD, and must supervise their own children.
  • 1st offense — $25 to the offending team/club; 2nd — $50; 3rd — the team forfeits its remaining matches and is not welcomed back.
  • Keep team belongings in your team area and out of the play space; clean up your area after you play, even if the trash isn’t yours.
