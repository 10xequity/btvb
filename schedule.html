FAQ

Good to know before you play.

Where are Boomtown volleyball tournaments held?

All Boomtown tournaments run at Boomtown FieldhouseUSA, 14200 E Alameda Ave, Aurora, CO 80012, serving the Denver metro on indoor courts.

What formats does Boomtown run?

Reverse Co-Ed 4s, gendered 6s, women's and gendered 4s, and short-court doubles — play follows USAV and AVP rule sets.

Are there membership fees?

No. There are no membership fees to play in Boomtown tournaments; you register per event through the schedule link.

Are events sanctioned?

Yes — Boomtown events run under USAV, AVP, and AAU, and we host USAV-sanctioned qualifier play.
